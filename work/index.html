this is all the music I've made. many of these are covers of existing songs, but some were fully made by me. they were all made with PICO-8.
if for whatever reason you want to download them, right-click or long tap on the player and choose “save audio as...”. they all have full metadata added, so they'll look nice in whatever app you use.
you have full right to do whatever with them except claim them as yours, and you must credit me somehow.

these songs were created for ｢In a Heartbeat｣, a game I almost finished, but never got around to it. it was supposed to be a visual novel dating sim made in LÖVE with PICO-8-inspired visuals.

LIFE by Neuro-sama

this is the first song where I encountered a unique issue: the song didn't fit in one PICO-8 cartridge. I solved this by simply putting the rest (just the last chorus) in a second cartridge and combining the two in Audacity.
I also discovered that PICO-8 can smoothly fade out sound. I can't do it in the audio editor – it's a runtime effect – but it's possible so I allow it.

MEGALOVANIA by Toby Fox

between covering ｢LIFE｣ and covering this song, I discovered what WAV files are – raw uncompressed audio. PICO-8 actually exports in WAV by default, I (dumbly) thought WAV was an obscure format that not many programs recognized. so, everything prior to this is MP3. at some point I'll dig up the original P8 files for the rest and reexport as WAV.
